Finally decided to break the silence and share my journey of moving from React to the Angular universe. It is my first ever post, so go easy on me, and feel free to share suggestions.

## Background

I started with the MERN stack, thanks to Programming Hero, and all the npm packages in the React world. But hold on, there is a plot twist at work. Enter Angular, PostgreSQL, and Hapi.js. The twist did not come empty handed. It brought its own set of challenges, yet it has been an incredible learning experience.

## Key technologies I am exploring

### Angular

Transitioning to Angular has introduced me to its powerful features, including a modular structure and TypeScript magic. It has been a refreshing experience crafting applications with this robust framework.

### PostgreSQL

Moving from MongoDB to PostgreSQL has been a deep dive into the world of relational databases. The data modeling capabilities and rich querying options have added a new dimension to my skill set.

### Hapi.js

Exploring server side development with Hapi.js has been an adventure. Its flexibility and ease of use have streamlined the backend development process.

## Gratitude and looking forward

I am grateful for the support of my team and mentors, especially Mashud Karim bhaia. Looking ahead, I am eager to contribute to impactful projects and explore new horizons.
